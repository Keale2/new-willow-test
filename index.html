  • Proposed effective date
  • Broker Contact Information and commission structure for each product line

Plan Information Requirements

  • Inforce Plan Design/if FTB, confirm there is no inforce coverage

Experience Requirements

< 250 Lives

No Experience Data Required

250–499 Lives

  • Total Paid Claims for experience period (2-5 years) (including Conversions and Pending claims, if available) up-to-date within 3 months of RFP
  • Waiver of Premium Claims (Face Amount for Base and Supp)

500+ Lives

  • Either Paid Premium OR Rate / Plan History for Experience Period (3-5 years)
  • Waiver of Premium Claims (Face Amount for Base and Supp)
  • Total Paid Claims (including Conversions and Pending claims, if available) up-to-date within 3 months of RFP
  • Disabled Lives
